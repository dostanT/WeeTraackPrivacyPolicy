Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

WeeTraack respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the WeeTraack mobile application.

Information We Collect

WeeTraack does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

  • There is no account registration, login, or user identification in the app.
  • All app data is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or any other personal data.

Subscriptions and Payments

WeeTraack offers two subscription options managed through Apple In-App Purchases using RevenueCat:

  • Monthly subscription
  • Yearly subscription

WeeTraack does not have access to your payment information. All transactions are processed securely by Apple and RevenueCat. For RevenueCat's privacy practices, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

Advertising

WeeTraack displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • AdMob may collect anonymized data to show ads.
  • If you allow App Tracking Transparency, ads may be personalized.
  • If you do not allow tracking, only non-personalized ads will be shown.
  • Data collection by AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

WeeTraack requests permission for App Tracking Transparency in accordance with Apple guidelines.

Tracking permission is optional and can be changed at any time in your device settings:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Notifications

WeeTraack may request permission to send notifications.

  • Notifications are used only for app functionality and reminders.
  • No notification data is collected or shared.

Data Storage

All app-related data is stored locally on your device.

WeeTraack does not use external servers or cloud storage for user data.

Children's Privacy

WeeTraack is suitable for all ages.

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children or adults.

Data Deletion

Since WeeTraack does not collect or store personal data, there is no data to delete from our side.

Removing the app from your device deletes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer:

  • Via the App Store page
  • Email: sup_dostan@outlook.com
